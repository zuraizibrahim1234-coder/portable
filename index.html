Life of General Zia before his takeover

This is a simple paragraph about the life of General Zia before his takeover.

General Zia-ul-haq was born in August of 1924 in Peshawar. He was educated at St.Stepehen's College in Delhi and was an army veteran in WW2, later fighting in the Indo-Pakistani War of 1965 which brought about name to his exceptional military talent. He defeated King hussein in the Jordanian Civil War with exemplary capability as a General and was rewarded by Zulfikar Ali Bhutto, the President of Pakistan at the time with the new position of "Chief of Army Staff". However, this was not an easy appointment as Zia was a low level General at that time and only after lengthening the list of candidates for the role by several times was he finally listed and chosen by Bhutto. After the contoversial Election of 1977, Zia took over pakistan in a Military Coup declaring Martial law. To prove his rightful intentions, he even went as far as having Bhutto hanged despite great pleads by several superpowers of the world such as the Unites States Of America and China.
